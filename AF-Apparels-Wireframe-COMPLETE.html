Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access wholesale pricing?
Create a free wholesale account by submitting your business information. Once approved (typically within 24 hours), you'll have full access to wholesale pricing, the bulk order grid, and your account dashboard. There's no fee and no commitment required to apply.
Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
No minimums on in-stock items — order 1 unit or 10,000, your choice. Pricing scales with volume automatically based on your cart quantity. Private label orders (custom styles and colors) have a minimum of 2,500 units per style per color.
Are your blanks compatible with DTF and screen printing?
Yes — all core blanks are tested for DTF transfers, screen printing, and embroidery. Each product page includes a Print Guide tab with compatibility ratings and recommended press settings per decoration method. Our ring-spun and CVC tees are consistently top-rated by our printing customers.
How fast do orders ship?
Orders placed before 2 PM CT ship the same day from our Dallas, TX warehouse. Standard ground delivery reaches most of the continental US in 2–5 business days. Expedited and freight options are available at checkout for time-sensitive or large-volume orders.
Can I order private label with my own branding?
Yes. Our Private Label program supports custom neck labels, tags, and branding on your own styles and colors. Minimum is 2,500 units per style per color with a 45–60 day lead time. Contact our sales team to discuss your private label requirements and get a quote.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, ACH bank transfer, and wire transfer. NET 30 payment terms are available for qualifying wholesale accounts with a consistent order history — apply through your account dashboard once established.

Bulk Order Grid — Logic Spec

Color Toggle
Click swatch → selected state (CSS box-shadow ring) → row appears in grid. Click again → deselected → row removed.
Grid Row
Color dot + name | qty inputs × 6 sizes | stock count below each input | row total | ✕ remove
Stock Count
Live API — not static. Input max = stock count. Red border if qty exceeds stock. Warn before submit.
Row Total
Sum of all 6 size qty inputs for that color. Recalculates on every keyup/change event.
Grand Total
Sum of all row totals across all selected colors.
Price/Unit ⚠
Tier logic: 1–49=$5.52 | 50–249=$4.99 | 250–999=$4.49 | 1000+=$3.99. Calculated silently. UI shows ONLY the resulting price — never the brackets.
Order Total
Grand Total × Price Per Unit. Updates live on every input change.
ATC Button
Disabled (grey, pointer-events:none) at 0 units. Active (red) when >0. Label: "ADD TO CART — [N] UNITS"

Decoration Method Guide

Press settings and compatibility ratings for all AF core blanks

🖨️

DTF Transfers

Direct-to-Film heat transfer

✅ Best Method
Temperature320–325°F (160–163°C)
Time12–15 seconds
PressureMedium (40–60 PSI)
Peel TypeCold peel (wait 10–15 sec)
Recommended FabricsRing-Spun Cotton, CVC Blend
Wash Durability50+ washes (standard settings)
Pro Tips from Our Print Shop Partners
  • Pre-press for 3–5 seconds to remove moisture before applying transfer
  • Allow garment to fully cool before cold peel — rushing causes edge lift
  • For dark garments: white underbase in the DTF print is critical
  • Store transfers flat and away from humidity for best shelf life
  • On CVC blends: reduce temp by 5°F vs 100% cotton
🎨

Screen Printing

Plastisol and water-based inks

✅ Best Method
Ink TypePlastisol (primary) / Water-based
Flash Temp275–300°F for 3–5 sec between colors
Cure Temp (Plastisol)320°F for 90 seconds minimum
Mesh Count110–160 for standard / 200+ for fine detail
Pre-TreatmentNone required on ring-spun cotton
Wash DurabilityExcellent — 75+ washes with proper cure
Pro Tips from Our Print Shop Partners
  • Ring-spun cotton's smooth surface delivers sharper halftone detail vs open-end
  • Water-based inks: test adhesion on CVC blends before production runs
  • Tight-knit construction reduces ink bleed on fine lines
  • No pre-press needed — fabric arrives print-ready from our facility
🌊

Sublimation

Dye sublimation printing

⚠️ Limited
⚠️ Important: Sublimation requires minimum 65% polyester content. AF core tees are cotton-heavy — sublimation is only suitable on white or very light colors, and results will be muted vs 100% polyester blanks. For sublimation runs, consider our Performance Polo (#5001) or contact us about polyester blanks.
Temperature380–400°F (193–204°C)
Time45–60 seconds
PressureMedium-heavy
Best Fabric#5001 Performance Polo (CVC 65/35)
Garment ColorWhite only for full vibrancy

When Sublimation Works on AF Blanks
  • White garments only — any base color will affect output
  • Use #5001 Performance Polo for best sublimation results

  • Test on a single unit before committing to a production run

Embroidery

Machine and hand embroidery

✅ Recommended
Backing TypeCutaway (designs >10k stitches)
ToppingSolvy/tearaway on textured fabrics
Needle Size75/11 or 80/12 for standard weight
Thread TensionStandard — no adjustment typically needed
Best PlacementLeft chest, sleeve, collar — flat areas
Stitch Count Max15,000 stitches without distortion
Pro Tips from Our Print Shop Partners
  • Ring-spun construction creates a stable base — minimal stabilizer distortion
  • Low-profile collar on tees sits flat under hooping for clean chest placement
  • Avoid embroidery over seams — use flat panel areas only
  • For fleece hoodies (#3001, #3002): use cutaway backing on all designs
  • Pique knit on polos (#5001): use tearaway backing for cleaner finish
🔲

HTV / Vinyl

Heat Transfer Vinyl

✅ Recommended
Temperature300–315°F (149–157°C)
Time10–15 seconds
PressureMedium
Peel TypeWarm peel (most HTV brands)
Recommended HTVSiser EasyWeed, ThermoFlex Plus
Wash Durability40–50+ washes with correct settings
Pro Tips from Our Print Shop Partners
  • Pre-press 3 seconds to remove moisture and wrinkles before applying
  • Smooth ring-spun surface = excellent adhesion vs open-end cotton
  • For multi-layer designs: allow full cool between each layer press
  • Avoid stretching areas (side seams) for long-term adhesion
  • On hoodies: press flat — avoid pressing over drawstrings
