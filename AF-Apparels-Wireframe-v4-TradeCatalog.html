Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a wholesale account?
Click "Apply for Wholesale Account," fill out the form with your business details and tax ID, and we'll approve your account within 1 business day. Once approved, you'll see wholesale pricing and can place orders immediately.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
No minimum on any in-stock style. Order 1 unit or 10,000 — same-day fulfillment applies to both. Private label programs require a minimum of 2,500 pcs per style per color.
Do you offer NET 30 payment terms?
Yes. NET 30 is available for approved wholesale accounts with 90+ days of order history. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, ACH, and Wire on all accounts from day one.
What's the cut-off for same-day shipping?
Orders placed before 2:00 PM Central Time, Monday through Friday, ship same day from our Dallas warehouse. Orders placed after 2PM or on weekends ship the next business day.
Are your fabrics suitable for DTF and screen printing?
Yes. All AF blanks are print-optimized — high-density stitch count for a smooth printing surface, pre-shrunk ring-spun cotton, and consistent weight run-to-run. Check our Print Guide for full compatibility by decoration method.
Can I get samples before placing a bulk order?
Yes. Wholesale account holders can order sample units at standard per-unit pricing (no sample surcharge). Samples ship same day with your regular account terms.

AF Apparels Print Guide

Decoration compatibility, fabric specs, and print recommendations for all AF blank styles.

Decoration Methods — AF Compatibility

All AF blanks are designed with decoration in mind. Here's what works best on each style.

🖨️ Screen Printing

Our most popular print method among wholesale accounts. High-density stitch count and pre-shrunk cotton ensures excellent ink adhesion and vibrant results.

Best fabrics: 100% Ring-Spun Cotton
Temp: 320°F cure / flash at 250°F
Works on: #1001, #1000, #1002, #4001

🎨 DTF Transfer

Direct-to-film transfers adhere exceptionally well to our ring-spun cotton styles. Smooth surface texture minimizes grain-through. Full-color, no minimums on transfers.

Best fabrics: 100% Cotton or CVC 60/40
Temp: 300–315°F · 10–15 seconds
Works on: All AF styles

🪡 Embroidery

All AF blanks are embroidery-ready. Polo (#5001) and crewneck (#4001) are particularly well-suited for logo and chest embroidery.

Best fabrics: All AF styles
Backing: Cutaway for stability
Best on: #5001 Polo, #4001 Crewneck

🌈 Sublimation

Polyester-blend styles only. Our CVC 60/40 (#1000) supports sublimation with moderate color vibrancy. 100% polyester not currently in AF lineup.

Compatible fabrics: CVC 60/40 only
Note: Colors less vibrant on 60/40 vs 100% poly
Works on: #1000 Max Tee only

🔥 HTV / Vinyl

Heat transfer vinyl works on all AF blanks. Cotton styles perform best with standard HTV pressure and temp settings. Pre-shrunk fabric prevents peel at seams.

Best fabrics: 100% Ring-Spun Cotton
Temp: 305°F · 10–15 seconds · medium pressure
Works on: All AF styles

💧 Water-Based / Discharge

Our 100% ring-spun cotton styles are ideal for water-based and discharge printing. High-quality cotton construction ensures soft hand feel after wash-down.

Best fabrics: 100% Ring-Spun Cotton only
Note: Avoid on CVC blends — poly content resists discharge
Best on: #1001, #1002

Shipping & Returns

📦 All in-stock orders placed before 2:00 PM Central Time, Monday–Friday, ship same day from our Dallas, TX warehouse.

Shipping Carriers & Timelines

We ship via UPS and FedEx Ground as default. Expedited options (2-day, overnight) are available at checkout. Carrier selection at our discretion for ground orders — contact us if you require a specific carrier or account number.

  • UPS/FedEx Ground: 2–7 business days depending on destination
  • 2-Day Air: 2 business days from ship date
  • Overnight: Next business day by 10:30AM or 3PM (specify at checkout)
  • International: Freight coordination available — contact us before ordering

Shipping Costs

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on order weight and destination ZIP. No handling surcharges. Free shipping is not currently offered — our factory-direct pricing model keeps product costs low in lieu of free shipping incentives.

Returns Policy

AF Apparels accepts returns on defective products only. We do not accept returns on correctly fulfilled orders due to buyer's change of order, sizing errors, or decoration errors made by the buyer's team.

  • Defective products must be reported within 14 days of delivery
  • Contact us at info.afapparel@gmail.com with order number, SKU, and photos of the defect
  • We will issue a full replacement or store credit — no restocking fee on defective returns
  • Return shipping for defective items is covered by AF Apparels

Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives visibly damaged, document with photos before opening and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will file the carrier claim and arrange replacement at no charge.

Ordering & Account Terms

💳 Wholesale accounts are required to place orders. Application is free and takes 2 minutes. Approval within 1 business day.

Account Application

All buyers must apply for a wholesale account. We require a valid business name, business email, and Tax ID or Reseller Certificate. Personal orders are not accepted. Account approval is at our discretion.

Pricing & Volume Tiers

Pricing is tiered by total units per order (not per SKU or per color):

  • 1–49 units: Standard wholesale price
  • 50–249 units: ~10% discount
  • 250–999 units: ~18% discount
  • 1,000+ units: ~28% discount — contact for custom quote

Payment Terms

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, ACH bank transfer, and Wire transfer. NET 30 terms are available for accounts with 90+ days of order history and good payment standing. NET 30 applications are reviewed quarterly.

Order Minimums

No minimum order quantity on in-stock styles. Private label orders require a minimum of 2,500 units per style per color.

Order Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled before they enter fulfillment (typically within 1–2 hours of placement). Once fulfilled and labeled for shipment, cancellations are not possible. Contact us immediately if a cancellation is needed.

Design System

Typography

  • Headings: Barlow Semi Condensed 700–800wt — institutional, trade catalog energy
  • Body: Inter 400/500 — clean, modern, readable
  • Never use: Oswald (fashion-coded), Roboto (generic), Impact (screaming)

Color Palette

Navy #1B3A5CHeader, hero, CTA sections
Red #E8242ACTAs, badges, accent only
Blue #1E90FFLinks, info, accents
White #FFFFFFBody sections (dominant)
Off-white #F4F6F9Alt sections, inputs
Dark #0A0A0AFooter only

Section Rhythm Rule

White → Off-white → White → Off-white. No dark sections in body. Dark = header, hero, CTA section, footer only. This is the SanMar pattern that reads as "reliable supplier" not "fashion brand."

Photography Brief

Product Photography

  • Primary: Ghost mannequin, pure white background, consistent lighting
  • Secondary: Flat lay on light grey surface — for color swatches
  • No lifestyle: No models in outdoor/lifestyle settings for catalog images
  • Color shots: Each color needs its own ghost mannequin or flat lay
  • Angles: Front, back, side, detail (fabric closeup), label/tag

Facility Photography

  • Exterior: Warehouse building, loading docks, Dallas location signage
  • Interior: Shelving racks with folded blanks organized by color, fulfillment team in action
  • Detail: Fabric closeup (texture), label/certification tags on garments
  • Team: Professional headshots if applicable — builds trust for B2B buyers

Photography Don'ts

  • No streetwear lifestyle shots in the catalog
  • No influencer-style imagery anywhere in product pages
  • No black backgrounds on product photos
  • No inconsistent lighting across colors of the same style
  • No watermarks on product images

Technical Implementation

Recommended Platform

  • B2B-specific: Shopify B2B (Shopify Plus) or BigCommerce B2B Edition
  • WAM-style: Custom build on WooCommerce + B2B plugin
  • Smartex uses WAM platform — functional but dated UI
  • Recommendation: Shopify B2B for easiest account gating, tier pricing, NET 30 management

Key B2B Features Needed

  • Account application + approval workflow

  • Bulk order grid (qty input per size/color on one screen)

  • Real-time inventory by size and color

QuickBooks Integration

  • Confirm with Faisal: QB Payments vs. QB Accounting API priority

Section Order Rules — What Cannot Move

Fixed Order (Homepage)

  • Announce bar → Header → Sub-nav → Hero → Cert strip → Trust strip → Categories → Products → Who We Serve → Why AF → Logos → FAQ → CTA → Footer
  • Cert strip MUST come before products — certifications build the trust that makes price believable
  • Trust strip MUST be white background directly after the navy hero — jarring if skipped

What Not To Do

  • Don't move certs to the footer — nobody sees them there
  • Don't use dark/black section backgrounds in the body of any page
  • Don't add a "lifestyle" hero banner replacing the 2-col product hero
  • Don't use Oswald font — it will immediately feel like a streetwear brand
  • Don't price-gate the homepage — products should be visible, pricing gated only
  • Don't add a chat widget that blocks the ATC on mobile product pages
